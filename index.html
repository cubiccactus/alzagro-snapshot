HOW CROPLER WORKS

EASY TAKE-OFF

With one simple click on your smartphone the drone takes off and recognizes the grain pile by itself and creates a sampling route

CONFIRM THE ROUTE

The route will be based on international sampling standards and on the amount of the actual grain pile. You can confirm or adjust the route.

AUTOMATIC SAMPLING

The drone takes samples across the sections of the pile, with the help of a vacuum system. The method takes about 10 seconds per sample.

IMMEDIATE RESULTS

Quick and accurate results based on the taken samples and the results of the NIR analyzation system, which include protein, moisture, gluten, and ash level measurements.
